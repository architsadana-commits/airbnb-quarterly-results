North America

Mid-single digit

Nights & Seats Booked growth. ADR +5% driven by mix shift. Reserve Now, Pay Later launched in Aug helped accelerate bookings.

EMEA

Mid-single digit

Nights & Seats Booked growth. Slight unfavorable Y/Y comp due to Paris Olympics in 2024. ADR +10% (4% ex-FX).

Latin America

Low-20s%

Nights & Seats Booked growth. Brazil origin nights +20%, first-time bookers +17%. Installment plans more than doubled booker uptake.

Asia Pacific

Mid-teens%

Nights & Seats Booked growth. Japan domestic travel +27% Y/Y (3rd consecutive quarter of acceleration). First-time bookers +50% in India.
